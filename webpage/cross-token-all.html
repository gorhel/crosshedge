What Is KYC?

KYC or ‘Know Your Customer’ is a customer identification process. Regulatory authorities across most jurisdictions require that token sellers acquaint themselves with purchasers of their tokens. The process is in place in order to prevent fraud, money laundering and identity theft, amongst others.

Cross Hedge KYC Process

Cross Hedge partnered with a reputable KYC/AML provider to ensure minimal disruption and inconvenience to the token purchaser. Cross Hedge KYC/AML platform allows you to complete your KYC formalities completely online, real time. Buyers have the capability to complete KYC and purchase BKX tokens contemporaneously.

Who Needs to Complete KYC Verification?

In short, all purchasers of BKX tokens, irrespective of amount or place of domicile, need to complete KYC. This includes:

  • Individual(s) or Non-Individual(s)
  • A guardian who is investing on behalf of a minor
  • Constituted Power of Attorney (PoA) holder(s)
  • Other (ask your attorney)
How Become KYC Compliant?

Complying with KYC norms is easy and a one-time process.
Purchasers who need to become KYC compliant can register directly on our Cross Hedge website.

What Documents Are Required for KYC?

Uploaded scanned copies of the following documents are required for completing the KYC compliance process:

  • Documents showing proof of identity such as a passport or a national ID.

Important Note

When Creating your account you will receive 2 keys: a Public key and a Secret key.
We recommend you keep them in a safe computer, better way is to write it in a safe place, so have your secret notebook ready. Be sure not to be spied at this stage… ;-)

  • Public key: The public key is used to identify the account. It is also known as an account.
    This public key is used for receiving funds.
  • Secret key: The secret key is used to access your account and make transactions.
    Keep this code safe and secure. Anyone with the code will have full access to the account and funds. If you lose the key, you will no longer be able to access the funds and there is no recovery mechanism.
Ready to start?

To create your account, you first have to get a Stellar Wallet.
You can chose whether a desktop Wallet or a web wallet.

  • Desktop Wallet: it is an app you will download and open on your computer. You will create your account, get your keys and will do all your transactions through it.
